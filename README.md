An AI powered roadmap to give you exactly what you need to learn. A direct replacement for spoon feeding. You decide what you want to learn, how you want to learn it, and this will give you the roadmap.

### Intented users

Just not for developers, but for everyone who wants to learn anything. A hobbysts like me, a philosopher who want's to learn about Marcus Aurelius, a architect who wants to sharpen their skills, a medical practitioner who wants to learn Python. Literally everyone.

### Constraints

- The app only works with your custom instruction through text.
- It does not support the use of images, audio (in the checklist), or video.

### Design

It is inspired by [material design of google](https://m3.material.io/). Particularly the [gemini](https://gemini.google.com/) interface. 

Reason being, this will induce some sense of familiarity for people, thus reducing the adjustment for people to understand the interface. More trust.
